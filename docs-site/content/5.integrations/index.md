# Integrations

Connect your Velvet waitlists to third-party services to automate workflows and streamline your operations.

## Available Integrations

::card-group
  ::card{title="Zapier" icon="i-logos-zapier" to="/integrations/zapier"}
  Automate workflows with 5,000+ apps including CRMs, email marketing tools, and more.
  ::
  
  ::card{title="Webhooks" icon="i-heroicons-globe-alt" to="/integrations/webhooks"}
